Lesson 19: DOM

Task 1.
We display the cards of goods that the user can choose.The selected item has a green frame
Task 2.
A list of athletes is given.You need to form a list of those that will participate in the competition.There are two columns.One shows all the athletes, the other is a list of those who were selected.When you click on the green arrow, the athlete moves on the list for competitions.When you click on the red arrow, the athlete moves on the general list
Task 3.
Display falling snow.The snowflake appears at the top of the screen and moves down at random speed.As soon as the snowflake reaches the bottom of the screen, it should appear at the top of the screen again.
Task 4.
Shining stars.The specified number of stars should appear in the random part of the screen.Each star with a certain step and interval increases from a minimum to maximum size.As soon as the maximum size, the asterisk should appear in another random position.
Task 5.
Baracar.From the top of the screen, in a random position horizontally appears tanks that go down.When click on the tank, it explodes and disappears from the screen.
