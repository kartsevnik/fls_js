Lesson 3: if...else expressions

Task 1.
The names of two children and the number of candies they have are entered from the keyboard. Print off the screen the name of the child whose whose number of candies is greater, or print that the number is the same.
Task 2.
Enter the price of the goods and the amount of money from the keyboard. If there is not enough money, we refuse to buy, otherwise, if there is still money left, we offer to buy the lottery for 4 UAH.
Task 3.
A number from 1 to 5 is randomly generated. Try to guess the number in 2 attempts
Task 4.
Enter the person's age from the keyboard. Display the person's age (kindergartner, schoolchild, student, worker, pensioner).
Task 5.
Enter the name of the driver category (A-motorcycle, B-car, C-truck) from the keyboard. Display the name of the vehicle that the driver can drive.
Task 6.
Use the keyboard to enter the day of the week. Display the name of the day.
